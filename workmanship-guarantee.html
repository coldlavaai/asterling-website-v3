Home/Workmanship Guarantee

1 Year Workmanship Guarantee

At A Sterling Landscapes Ltd, we take pride in the quality of our work. That's why every completed landscaping project is backed by our 12-month workmanship guarantee.

If any issue arises as a direct result of our installation or workmanship within 12 months of completion, we'll return to inspect the problem and, where covered, put it right at no additional labour cost.

What Our Guarantee Covers

  • Installation defects caused by our workmanship
  • Movement or failure resulting from incorrect installation
  • Faults with patios, paving, decking, fencing, pergolas, turf and other landscaping features installed by our team, where the issue is due to workmanship

What Our Guarantee Does Not Cover

  • Natural movement or settlement of the ground
  • Damage caused by severe weather, flooding, storms or other events outside our control
  • Accidental damage, misuse, neglect or alterations made by others
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by tree roots, invasive plants or ground movement
  • Natural characteristics of timber, including shrinking, warping, splitting, knots, resin bleed or colour changes due to weathering
  • Materials supplied by the customer (unless a workmanship fault has caused the issue)

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering landscaping that is built to last and providing excellent aftercare for all of our customers.

Your satisfaction is important to us, and we'll always work with you to resolve any genuine workmanship issues promptly and professionally.

To raise an issue covered by this guarantee, contact us on 07795 599 909 with your name, the project address and a description (photos help) and we will arrange an inspection.
