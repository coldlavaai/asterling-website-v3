Privacy Policy

Introduction

A Sterling Landscapes Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect any information you provide when using this website or contacting us directly.

A Sterling Landscapes operates in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who We Are

A Sterling Landscapes Ltd
Chichester, West Sussex
Phone: 07795 599 909
Website: asterling.co.uk

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us using the details above.

What Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location or postcode
  • Information provided through contact forms or the chatbot
  • Project enquiry details
  • Website usage data (anonymous)

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide quotations
  • Manage project communications
  • Provide information about our services
  • Improve the website and user experience

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How Your Information Is Stored

Your personal data is stored securely and only retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to protect your data.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing

Requests can be made by contacting A Sterling Landscapes using the details above.

Third Party Services

This website may use third party services such as contact forms, chatbots, analytics tools or embedded maps. These providers may collect limited information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in legal requirements or website functionality.
