Bespoke Fencing & Gates

Boundaries that define your space.

Boundaries

Boundaries That Define Your Space

Fencing and gates serve a practical purpose — privacy, security and boundary definition. But at A Sterling Landscapes, they also serve a design purpose. Well-crafted fencing frames a garden, creates structure and sets the tone for everything within it.

We design and install fencing and gates that are built to last, look exceptional and complement the wider landscape design.

Options

Fencing Options

A Sterling Landscapes offers a comprehensive range of fencing styles:

  • Bespoke Fencing — Custom-designed panels, screens and enclosures tailored to your garden
  • Close Board — Traditional, robust and enduring — the standard of quality timber fencing
  • Composite Fencing — Low-maintenance, weather-resistant and available in contemporary finishes
  • Picket Fencing — Classic charm for front gardens and boundary features
  • Post and Rail — Open, rural-style fencing for larger properties and paddocks
  • Enclosed Fencing — Full-privacy panels for gardens that demand seclusion
Gates

Bespoke Gate Design

A gate is the threshold between the outside world and your private garden. A Sterling Landscapes creates bespoke gates that make a statement — whether that is a sleek contemporary entrance or a traditional timber gate with hand-forged ironwork.

Every gate is designed to complement the fencing, the property and the garden it protects.

Durability

Built to Withstand

Fencing is exposed to the elements year-round. A Sterling Landscapes uses premium timber, composite materials and professional fixings that ensure every panel, post and rail stands strong through seasons of wind, rain and sun. Proper post foundations and precise installation mean your fencing will not lean, warp or deteriorate prematurely.
