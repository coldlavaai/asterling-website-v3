Groundworks & Drainage

The invisible foundation of every great garden.

Foundations

The Invisible Foundation of Every Great Garden

The most important work in any landscaping project is often the work you cannot see. Proper groundworks and drainage are the foundation upon which everything else is built — and cutting corners here means problems later.

A Sterling Landscapes approaches groundworks with the same precision and commitment to quality that defines our finished surfaces. Because a garden that looks exceptional must also perform exceptionally.

Photo: Excavator on site during groundworks phase
Groundworks

Groundworks & Foundations

The team handles all aspects of site preparation and groundworks:

  • Site Clearance — Removal of existing structures, vegetation and waste
  • Excavation — Precision excavation for patios, driveways, walls and structures
  • Foundations — Concrete strip, pad and raft foundations for walls, cabins and pergolas
  • Sub-Base Preparation — Compacted aggregate bases for paving, driveways and artificial grass
  • Levelling and Grading — Establishing correct falls and levels for drainage and visual quality

A Sterling Landscapes operates a comprehensive range of machinery — from mini excavators and dumpers to compaction equipment — ensuring that groundworks are completed efficiently and to specification.

Photo: Drainage channel being installed
Drainage

Drainage Solutions

Effective drainage is essential for any outdoor space. Standing water damages surfaces, undermines foundations and renders gardens unusable after rainfall. A Sterling Landscapes designs and installs drainage systems that manage surface water effectively:

  • Land drains and French drains
  • Channel drainage for patios and driveways
  • Soakaway construction
  • Connection to existing drainage infrastructure
  • Permeable paving solutions

Every drainage scheme is designed to handle the specific conditions of your site — soil type, gradient, water table and rainfall patterns.

Repair Work

A Sterling Landscapes also undertakes repair and remediation work on existing landscaping — from resetting sunken paving and rebuilding retaining walls to addressing drainage failures.
