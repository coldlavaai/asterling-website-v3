Driveways

The First Impression Your Home Deserves

A driveway is the first thing visitors see and the last thing they remember. At A Sterling Landscapes, we believe it should make a statement — one that reflects the quality of the home it leads to.

We design and install driveways that combine visual impact with lasting durability. From sweeping resin-bound surfaces to intricate block paving patterns and natural stone approaches, every driveway is tailored to the property and the client.

Materials

Driveway Materials & Finishes

A Sterling Landscapes works across the full spectrum of premium driveway materials:

  • Block Paving — Classic, versatile and available in a wide range of colours and patterns
  • Resin-Bound — Smooth, permeable and contemporary with a seamless finish
  • Natural Stone — Granite, sandstone and limestone for a distinguished appearance
  • Tarmac — Professional-grade tarmac with clean edging for a sharp, durable finish
  • Shingle & Gravel — Elegant and practical, ideal for period properties
  • Concrete — Modern exposed aggregate and polished finishes
  • Cobbles — Traditional cobblestone for character and heritage appeal
Engineering

Engineered for Performance

A beautiful driveway must also perform. A Sterling Landscapes designs every driveway with proper drainage, load-bearing capacity and surface water management built in from the start.

The team handles all groundworks, sub-base preparation and edging — ensuring that the finished surface sits perfectly and performs reliably for years to come.

Additional Services

Drop Kerb Installation

Where required, A Sterling Landscapes manages the full drop kerb process, including liaison with local authorities. This ensures seamless access from road to driveway without compromising the design.
