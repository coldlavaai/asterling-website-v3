Luxury Decking

Extend your living space outdoors.

Outdoor Living

Extend Your Living Space

Decking creates an elevated outdoor living area that bridges the gap between your home and garden. When designed and built to the highest standard, it becomes an extension of your interior — a space for dining, entertaining and quiet enjoyment.

A Sterling Landscapes designs and builds decking that is structurally sound, visually striking and tailored to the way you want to live.

Materials

Decking Materials

The choice of material determines the look, feel and longevity of your deck:

Composite Decking

Engineered for durability, low maintenance and consistent colour. Resistant to rot, fade and slip. The modern choice for luxury outdoor living.

Hardwood Decking

Rich, natural character with exceptional strength. Species such as Ipe, Cumaru and Iroko offer warmth and elegance that improves with age.

Softwood Decking

A cost-effective option that, when treated and maintained, provides a beautiful and functional outdoor surface.

A Sterling Landscapes will advise on the best material for your project based on design intent, budget and maintenance preference.

Bespoke Design

Designed for Your Space

Every deck is designed around the garden and the property. The team considers levels, sightlines, access points and how the decking integrates with other features such as planting, lighting, steps and balustrades.

Multi-level decks, wrap-around designs and integrated seating areas are all within the team's capability — creating outdoor spaces that feel considered, luxurious and entirely bespoke.

Quality

Structural Integrity

A Sterling Landscapes builds decking on properly engineered subframes with appropriate foundations. This ensures that every board sits level, every fixing is secure and the deck performs safely and beautifully for years to come.
