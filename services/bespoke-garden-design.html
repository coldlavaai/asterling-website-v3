Bespoke Garden Design

Your vision, expertly realised.

Design & Build

Your Vision, Expertly Realised

The most remarkable gardens begin with a bold vision. A Sterling Landscapes works with homeowners, architects and garden designers to take that vision from concept to reality — delivering complete garden transformations that redefine outdoor living.

Whether you arrive with detailed plans from a designer or an idea sketched on the back of an envelope, the team has the expertise and experience to bring it to life.

Collaboration

Working to Plans and Designs

A Sterling Landscapes has extensive experience working with professional garden designers, architects and landscape architects. The team can interpret technical drawings and translate design intent into precise, high-quality construction.

This collaborative approach ensures that the designer's vision is honoured while benefiting from the practical expertise of a team that understands what works in the ground.

Full Service

Complete Garden Transformations

For clients seeking a fully bespoke experience, A Sterling Landscapes offers complete design-and-build packages:

  • Initial site visit and consultation
  • Understanding of budget, timescale and aspirations
  • Design concept development
  • Material selection and specification
  • Full construction programme
  • Planting and finishing
  • Aftercare guidance

The result is a garden that feels cohesive, considered and entirely personal — every element working together to create something exceptional.

Ambition

Challenging Projects Welcome

A Sterling Landscapes actively seeks projects that push boundaries. Complex sites, ambitious designs, multiple levels, integrated water features, outdoor living spaces — these are the projects that showcase what the team can truly achieve. If your project exceeds £100,000, you are exactly the kind of client we want to work with.
