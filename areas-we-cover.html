Coverage

Premium Landscaping Across the South

A Sterling Landscapes is based in Chichester, West Sussex, and serves homeowners across a wide area of southern England. Our core coverage includes West Sussex, East Sussex and Surrey — with the flexibility to travel further for projects that match our ambitions.

For larger projects, we are happy to travel up to 100 miles from our base.

Locations

Our Core Areas

We regularly work in the following areas:

  • Chichester
  • Emsworth
  • Worthing
  • Brighton
  • Havant
  • Bognor Regis
  • Guildford
  • Petersfield
  • Littlehampton
  • West Sussex (County-Wide)

We also regularly serve Bosham, Fishbourne, Nutbourne, West Wittering, East Wittering, Southbourne, Pagham, Fontwell, Barnham, Bedhampton and Waterlooville.

Flexibility

Flexibility for Exceptional Projects
